About course

Course "Frontend Basics. HTML and CSS" is an important first step for mastering the profession of a front-end developer. On the course, we will go from getting acquainted with the general principles of web project layout to creating an adaptive landing page.

Who will be useful

Web designers, web developers, frontend developers.

Who teach

Anton Suprun is a leading developer of CASES and Creative Practice.

He has been engaged in frontend development since 2006, worked at Ciklum and for 11 years was the CTO and partner in the POPEL Agency agency, and now he is working on the technical development of the CASES social network and the Creative Practice educational platform.

He has more than 15 years of experience in creating web projects - from simple landing pages to complex web platforms.

Anton also has experience in frontend training as a mentor and as a teacher. He taught frontend at the School of Visual Communication.

This course consists of theoretical and practical modules. Each of the modules is devoted to a specific topic. All course materials are well structured, written in simple language, and laid out consistently.
